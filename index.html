Bienvenidos

A neighborhood cantina by the sea, right here in Nolensville

Cabos is named for Cabo San Lucas, where the desert meets the ocean at the famous El Arco. We bring that bright, sunny Baja spirit to Middle Tennessee with recipes made from scratch every day.

From our sizzling fajitas and slow-cooked birria to hand-crafted margaritas from a full bar, every plate is prepared with authentic ingredients, traditional techniques, and a whole lot of heart. Pull up a chair, grab a basket of warm chips, and stay a while.
